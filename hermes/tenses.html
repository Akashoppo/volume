Tenses

There are total twelve possible tenses in english.

  • Simple Present Tense

  1. S+V1+O

  2. S+do/does+not+V1+O

  3. Do/does+S+V1+O

  • Present Continuous Tense

  1. S+be+V1+ing+O

  2. S+be+not+V1+ing+O

  3. be+S+V1+ing+O

  • Present Perfect Tense

  1. S+has/have+been+O

    2. or

    S+has/have+V3+O


  2. S+has/have+not+been+O

    3. or

    S+has/have+not+V3+O


  3. Have/has+S+been+O

    4. or

    Have/has+S+V3+O

  • Present Perfect Continuous Tense

  1. S+have/has+been+Ving

  2. S+have/has+not+been+Ving

  3. Have/has+S+been+Ving

  • Simple Past Tense

  1. S+tobe(was/were)+O

    2. or

    S+V2+O


  2. S+tobe(was/were)+not+O

    3. or

    S+did not+V1+O


  3. Did+S+V1+O

  • Past Continuous Tense

  1. S+tobe(was/were)+Ving+O

  2. S+tobe(was/were)+not+Ving+O

  3. was/were+S+Ving+O

  • Past Perfect Tense

  1. S+had+V3+O

  2. S+had+not+V3+O

  3. Had+S+V3+O

  • Past Perfect Continuous Tense

  1. S+had+been+Ving+O

  2. S+had+not+been+Ving+O

  3. Had+S+been+Ving+O

  • Simple Future Tense

  1. S+will/shall+V1+O

    2. or

    S+am/is/are+goingto+V1+O


  2. S+will/shall+not+V1+O

    3. or

    S+am/is/are+not+goingto+V1+O


  3. will/shall+S+V1+O

    4. or

    Am/is/are+S+goingto+V1+O

  • Future Continuous Tense

  1. S+will/shall+be+Ving+O

  2. S+will/shall+not+be+Ving+O

  3. will/shall+S+be+Ving+O

  • Future Perfect Tense

  1. S+shall/will+have+been+O

  2. S+shall/will+not+have+been+O

  3. Shall/will+S+have+been+O

  • Future Perfect Continuous Tense

  1. S+shall/will+have+been+Ving+O

  2. S+shall/will+not+have+been+Ving+O

  3. Shall/will+S+have+been+Ving+O
